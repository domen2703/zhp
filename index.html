Online trenerstvoOnline coaching

Kot tvoj online coach ti zagotovim strokovno usmerjanje in motivacijo, da dosežeš svoje cilje na področju fitnesa, zdrave prehrane in življenjskega sloga — kjerkoli si. As your online coach I give you expert guidance and motivation to hit your fitness, nutrition and lifestyle goals — wherever you are.

You can simply call me. 15 or 30-minute consults: weight loss, body transformation, concrete nutrition and training questions, exercise selection and order.
